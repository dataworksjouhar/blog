I've wanted my own blog for a long time — a couple of years, honestly. The idea
was always the same: a place to write about what I'm learning, document the
projects I build, and occasionally reflect on the lessons that come from work and
life. This is that place.

## What this is

Think of it as a digital notebook that happens to be public. Most of what I write
will be about **data and AI** — notes from courses and certifications, written
simply enough that someone preparing for the same exam can actually follow along.
Alongside that, I'll document **projects** as I build them, and now and then share
a **reflection** on something less technical.

## How I'll write it

One post at a time, usually on a Saturday. I'm keeping the bar deliberately low —
short and useful beats long and perfect, because the thing that matters most is
showing up every week.

If something here helps you learn faster, or skip a mistake I made, then it's
doing its job. Thanks for reading the first one.
